Poché (the substance of architectural form) is a plastic material-space to imagine and inhabit.

Deep floor systems (embedded with space) form intimate rooms and baths for the scale of the body.

An architecture of mysterious shadow between spaces (such as two streets or a floor above a roof).

The floor defines spatial pockets (that share light, sound and glimpses of life).

Poché is (outside) space that is more interior than a room.

The floor creates dialogue between intimate and shared spaces.
